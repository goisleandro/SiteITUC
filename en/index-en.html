the ituc

The Technological Institute of PUC-Rio, ITUC, opened on April 12, 1959 under the name of Technological Institutes, has 61 years of activities dedicated to the provision of technological services to industry and other sectors of the economy.

Our structure comprises two units in Rio de Janeiro, which house a qualified and multidisciplinary technical staff and our own network of laboratories. We have researchers, technicians, engineers, teachers, consultants and administrative staff prepared to seek excellent results for the most diverse segments.
